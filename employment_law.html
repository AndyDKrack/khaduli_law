Employment Law

Navigating the complex landscape of employment law requires expertise, diligence, and a deep understanding of both legal statutes and human dynamics. At Khaduli & Asa Associates Advocates, we offer comprehensive employment law services to protect the rights of both employers and employees.

For employers

As an employer, ensuring compliance with employment laws is essential to maintaining a harmonious workplace and safeguarding your business interests. Our team of experienced attorneys provides tailored solutions to help you navigate the intricacies of employment law, including:

Employment Contracts: Drafting, reviewing, and negotiating employment contracts to protect your interests and ensure clarity for both parties.

Employee Handbooks: Developing comprehensive employee handbooks that outline company policies, procedures, and expectations in accordance with relevant laws and regulations.

Discrimination and Harassment: Advising on discrimination and harassment claims, providing proactive strategies for prevention, and representing your interests in legal proceedings when necessary.

Wage and Hour Compliance: Assisting with wage and hour compliance, including classification of employees, overtime pay, and minimum wage requirements.

Termination and Severance: Guiding employers through the process of termination and severance agreements to mitigate potential legal risks and ensure compliance with applicable laws.

For employees

Employees deserve fair treatment and protection of their rights in the workplace. Our dedicated team advocates for the rights of employees in various employment law matters, including:

Wrongful Termination: Representing employees in cases of wrongful termination, retaliation, and constructive dismissal to seek justice and fair compensation.

Discrimination and Harassment Claims: Providing legal counsel and representation to employees facing discrimination or harassment based on protected characteristics such as race, gender, age, disability, or sexual orientation.

Wage and Hour Disputes: Pursuing claims for unpaid wages, overtime violations, misclassification, and other wage and hour disputes to ensure employees receive fair compensation for their work.

Family and Medical Leave: Assisting employees in understanding their rights under the Family and Medical Leave Act (FMLA) and advocating for their rights in cases of FMLA violations or interference.

Employment Contract Review: Reviewing employment contracts, non-compete agreements, and severance packages to advise employees on their rights and negotiate favorable terms.

Why choose us?

At Khaduli & Asa Associates Advocates, we combine legal expertise with a client-centric approach to deliver personalized solutions tailored to your unique needs and circumstances. Whether you're an employer seeking to protect your business interests or an employee facing workplace challenges, you can trust our team to provide knowledgeable guidance and dedicated advocacy every step of the way.
